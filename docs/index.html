Privacy Policy

Sunny Coffee  ·  Last updated: April 7, 2026

Sunny Coffee ("the app") is a map application that helps you find sun-exposed cafés in Copenhagen, Denmark. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

The app requests access to your device's location for the following purpose:

  • Showing your current position on the map
  • Calculating distances to nearby cafés
  • Centering the map near you when you open the app

Location data is used exclusively on your device in real time. It is never stored, transmitted to our servers, or shared with any third party.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

Sunny Coffee does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any personal information of any kind

3. Third-Party Services

The app loads map tiles and building data from the following external services:

  • OpenFreeMap — provides the base map vector tiles. Tile requests contain only the map viewport bounding box, not your location.
  • OpenStreetMap / Overpass API — café and building data is fetched from the public Overpass API. Requests contain only the map viewport bounding box, not your location.

4. Location Permission

The app requests "When In Use" location permission only. You can deny or revoke this permission at any time in your iPhone's Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Sunny Coffee. The app remains usable without location access; the map will simply default to central Copenhagen.

5. Data Retention

Café data fetched from OpenStreetMap is cached locally on your device (in the app's private storage) to reduce network requests. This cache contains only public café information from OpenStreetMap — no personal data — and can be cleared by deleting and reinstalling the app.

6. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. It contains no account creation, in-app purchases, or social features.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
