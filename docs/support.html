Common Questions

Why is the map empty?

Sunny Coffee currently shows cafés in Copenhagen, Denmark only. If you are outside Copenhagen the map will appear empty — this is expected.

Why do I need to allow location access?

Location is used to show your position on the map and sort cafés by distance. You can deny location access and the app will still work — it will simply default to central Copenhagen.

The shadows look wrong

Shadow positions are calculated based on the time you set in the app and building data from OpenStreetMap. Accuracy depends on OSM building data being up to date. If you notice a specific building is missing or has the wrong height, you can contribute to OpenStreetMap.
